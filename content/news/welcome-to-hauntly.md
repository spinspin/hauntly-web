# Welcome to Hauntly

We're thrilled to announce the launch of **Hauntly**, the most advanced ghost hunting application ever built for mobile devices. Whether you're a seasoned paranormal investigator or a curious beginner, Hauntly puts powerful detection tools right in your pocket.

## What Makes Hauntly Different?

Unlike other ghost hunting apps, Hauntly combines multiple detection methods into a single, intuitive interface:

- **EMF Detection** — Uses your phone's magnetometer to detect electromagnetic field anomalies commonly associated with paranormal activity
- **EVP Recorder** — Captures and enhances audio to reveal electronic voice phenomena
- **Spirit Radar** — Our proprietary scanning system that maps potential paranormal hotspots in your surroundings
- **Investigation Journal** — Document your findings with photos, notes, and automatic environmental data logging

## Getting Started

Download Hauntly from the App Store or Google Play and create your free account. The app will walk you through a quick tutorial to help you understand each tool and how to use them effectively during investigations.

Hauntly isn't just an app — it's a community of paranormal enthusiasts. Share your findings, discuss experiences, and learn from other investigators around the world.
